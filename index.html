Our mission

We believe that social media interaction doesn't have to be superficial and a show-off. We're bringing interest-centric interaction back by providing you a secure and private way of matching with others based on your interests!

How it works

  • Select Interests

    When signing up, you can customize your profile with interests and personalize your bio.

  • Match and Chat

    Based on your interests and topics you'd like to talk about, we'll match you with a peer with the same interest.

  • Open up and make friends!

    While new chats use pseudonyms, you can open up during the conversation, share your profile and make new friends!
